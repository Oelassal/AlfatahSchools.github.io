All About Us

Alfatah's schools were established on a personal initiative in establishing private schools for girls next to the campus. Hence Makkah saw the emergence of private schools to be the first in the education of women, before the establishment of the General Presidency eighteen years - in 1380 AH. The schools were opened as "Al-Fattah Schools for Culture and Industry in Makkah Al-Mushrifah" and are the second private schools, which were opened during the reign of the founder King Abdul Aziz bin Abdul Rahman. It was founded by Sheikh Mohammed Hussein Bin Abdul Ghani Felemban. Science and knowledge of the present day.

Founder's Message

Alfatah private schools in Makkah is an ancient name and a distinguished educational edifice in girls' education in Saudi Arabia since 1367. Since then, it has been keen to prepare students intellectually, educationally and skillfully to contribute in building society with their commitment to values.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Vision: The first educational institution for the girl scientifically leading, intellectually conscious, global competition. Mission: Enable students to work with their learning and leave a positive impact locally and globally. And support in the development of the skills of students linguistic Arabic and English. And attention to mental, health and psychological fitness. Values: motivation, responsibility, learning by doing, happiness, charity, leadership. Teaching main ethics and principles to achieve the main goal of having a brighter era of creative and smart students for brighter future of knowledge.
